HTML LAYOUT

style sheet

HTML permits any number of STYLE elements in the HEAD section of a document. User agents that don't support style sheets, or don't support the specific style sheet language used by a STYLE element, must hide the contents of the STYLE element. It is an error to render the content as part of the document's text.

London

Lis the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.
